  4. Click on the 6x6 document
  5. Type 5in in the first line
  6. Type 5in in the second line
  7. Click Enter
  8. Center the 5x5in square
  9. Click on the rectangle tool
  10. Click on the 6x6in document
  11. Type 4in in the first line
  12. Type 4in in the second line
  13. Click Enter
  14. Center the 4x4in square
  15. Select the 4x4in square
  16. Click the "Object" tag at the top of the screen
  17. Hover over the "Transform" option under the "Object tag"
  18. Click the "Rotate..." option under the "Transform" tag
  19. Type 45 into the dialouge box
  20. Click Enter
  21. Select the 5x5in square
  22. Double click the color fill box to the left of the screen
  23. Type 1B75BC into the dioluge box with the hashtag infront of it
  24. Click Enter
  25. Select the 4x4in square
  26. Double click the color fill box to the left of the screen
  27. Type 27AAE1 into the dioluge box with the hashtag infront of it
  28. Click Enter
  29. Click the "File" tag at the top of the screen
  30. Hover over the "Export" option under the "File" tag
